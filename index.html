Hi, I'm Jenelle.

Computer science student, web developer, designer.

Web Development

As of April 2021, I have around 3 years professional experience with web development and design, having worked for both a branch of Colorado State University and a local marketing agency. I primarily work with client-friendly content management systems like WordPress and Shopify, although I occasionally get to build simple web projects from scratch, for which I enjoy using Jekyll.

I have around 2 years of professional experience building WordPress sites, using various page builders including Divi and Oxygen (my favorite!) as well as the Gutenberg editor. I have coded custom themes, PHP functions, and JavaScript/jQuery interactions.

Web Design

As of April 2021, I have around 3 years professional experience in web design. Personally, I have been seriously designing websites and web projects for about 5 years, and creating digital art and graphic designs for around 10 years.

I am highly skilled with a variety of different prototyping, illustration, and design softwares, including Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator/XD and Figma.

User experience design is my passion. Getting to create beautiful user interfaces that are built around the users it was designed for just gets me all warm and fuzzy inside. I currently am studying part time to pursue this passion of user experience design. I am pursuing my bachelor's of computer science with a concentration in human-centered computing at Colorado State University (estimated graduation 2022).
