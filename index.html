Hi, I'm Jenelle.

Front end developer & UI/UX Designer.

Web Development

As of December January 2021, I have around 3 years professional experience with web development and design, having worked for both a branch of Colorado State University and a local marketing agency. I primarily work with client-friendly content management systems like WordPress and Shopify, although I occasionally get to build simple web projects from scratch, for which I enjoy using Jekyll.

JavaScript is my main tool of the trade. I have an excellent handle on DOM manipulation with native JavaScript and also enjoy using jQuery to make my web design come to life. I am well-versed in using Ajax to create asynchronous pages and elements for sites. I am always honing my skills with object oriented and functional programming. Lately, React has been my framework of choice.

UI/UX Design

As of January 2021, I have around 3 years professional experience in UI/UX design. Personally, I have been seriously designing websites and web projects for about 5 years, and creating digital art and graphic designs for around 10 years.

I am highly skilled with a variety of different prototyping, illustration, and design softwares, including Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator/XD and Figma.

User experience design is my passion. Getting to create beautiful user interfaces that are built around the users it was designed for just gets me all warm and fuzzy inside. I currently am studying part time to pursue this passion of user experience design. I am pursuing my bachelor's of computer science with a concentration in human-centered computing at Colorado State University (estimated graduation 2022).

Education

I am currently studying Computer Science part-time with a concentration in Human-Centered Computing at Colorado State University. My expected graduation date is 2023.
